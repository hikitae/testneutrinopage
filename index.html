About Neutrino

Neutrino is a highly accurate fluid solver, currently used simulate coastal inundation and flooding scenarios but extensible to support various fluid dynamic flows.

Neutrino is developed independently by Neutrino Dynamics Initiative. It is composed of a group of diverse scientific research team distributed around the world. Its currently used by research organizations, academia, various industries and features a full-featured simulation and visualization environment for the most demanding tasks and problems. Neutrino has proven its versatility and efficiency in many diverse sectors, including energy research, environmental sciences , nuclear engineering and risk analysis.
